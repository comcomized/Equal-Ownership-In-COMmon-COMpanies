Belong by Owning your Equal Share With Us. This site is equally owned by groups which are equally owned by their members.

ComComIzed means: Turned into a common company in this licensed ownership over the company’s assets. This license guarantees equal, moderated and trade-able shareholding in unchangeable license, as in open coding.  The mobility and equality is kept in multiple layers, making the bigger unit stable even in restructuring of its smaller units, while some inequality still can be contained: between groups, by the factor d, and/or in different contracting and/or salaries. Altogether, making the group and the member live in a new healthier balance.

The groups in comcomized.com, when are ComComized, can have their sub-domain (see on the right side). Welcome and join us as you are, we begin as a consultancy for well produced bottom up media in liquid union channels in Europe, which  operates on this mailing list, while we are formed in this structuring.

Why is this site comcomized? To increase the collective value of mutual trust in your networks (your social capital) without the (so corruptible) position of winner take all.

For just being human – as not treating the other as objects, here nobody has a price. We are taking this constitutional approach against breaking equality and/or taken over, while we are free to earn and move in our struggle against constantly increasing volume of changes in laws and regulations (and their agents) benefiting only the big ones monopolizing the narratives and politics of privileges, the who and the what-could-be, the dealers and the deals, of real-estate/raw-material, weapon/security, media/market, and money/debt.
